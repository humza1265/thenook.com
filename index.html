The Philosophy

"A space designed for long conversations, slow mornings, creative afternoons and unforgettable evenings."

The Nook is Bandra's answer to a question the city has been asking: where do you go when you need a place that's neither home nor office? Somewhere the light is good, the coffee is better, and no one minds if you stay.

Founded by film-designer Rashi Morbia, every corner of The Nook — café tables by morning light, the bar at golden hour, the deep lounge after dark — is crafted to make you linger.

A day at The Nook

From first light
to last call.

Morning · 10 AM – 1 PM

The Café opens.

The smell of fresh ground coffee. Warm light through the east windows. Tables filling one at a time with people who know how to take their time with a morning.

Afternoon · 1 PM – 4 PM

Long lunches. Deep work.

Laptops open. Notebooks out. The steady hum of curated ambient sound. Plates of small plates, second (and third) coffees. The afternoon stretches pleasantly.

Golden Hour · 4 PM – 7 PM

The bar stirs awake.

The light shifts amber. The first cocktails appear. Conversations deepen. The day's best hour, claimed at The Nook with friends you've been meaning to catch up with.

Night · 7 PM – Close

As the sun goes down, the cocktails come out.

The lounge fills. A DJ settles in. The Smoke & Soil arrives at your table. You check your watch and decide to stay for one more.
